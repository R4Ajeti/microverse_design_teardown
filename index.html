Known as @desandro on Twitter, David DeSandro is a designer and developer based in Alexandria, VA. He runs Metafizzy, a small web development shop that has been building and supporting delightful JavaScript plugins and logos for over five years now — you might have heard of Flickity and Isotope already, for example. He’s also the creator of the cascading grid layout library Masonry which is in use all over the web. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear David!
